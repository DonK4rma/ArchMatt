FAQ

What's ArchMatt+?

ArchMatt+ is a Mod that adds 1024x Resolution Material for working on Little Tiles Structures with SEUS PTGI Support.

What's ArchMatt Lite?

ArchMatt Lite is a Resource Pack intended for visualisation of ArchMatt+ Build Converted to Vanilla; you can use this without needing ArchMatt+.

How do I Apply block.propeties on SEUS PTGI E12

Step 1. Make sure you backup your SEUS PTGI Build.

Step 2. Download the "block.propeties" file an extract it.

Step 3. Open your SEUS PTGI Build with an Compression Programm that allows you to modify compressed files.

Step 4. Replace the existing block.propeties with the download one.

Step 5. Profit.

Wrong Emmision on Blocks/Other Lighting Issues.

Step 1. Set Shaders to Off or Internal(Recommended).

Step 2. Reload your SEUS PTGI Build.

Din't Work?

Step 1.Relaunch your Client with Off or Internal(Recomended) Shaders.

Step 2. Open your World and then Set your SEUS PTGI Build.

I Have and Issue, Where can I submit it?

You can submit any Issues on ArchMatt+ GitHub Issues.
